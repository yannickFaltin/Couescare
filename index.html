Therapp helps bridge the gap between you and therapists.

Therapy can be pricey, and knowing if you need one can be tricky. our chatbot is here to help you find peace or the right treatment for you!

How it works:

You Feel Lost?

It is completely normal to feel lost, or unsure about why you are feeling the way you feel.

Our Bot Helps.

Our bot Thera is there to help. Asking general questions about emotions and mood, Thera is able to narrow down your pain points and either help you find clarity, or refer you to the right therapist you.

Gain Mental Clarity

After narrowing down and helping you become more aware of your emotions, you are able to return to a healthy life, where worries become an issue of the past.
